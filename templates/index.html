Welcome to our Movie Database!

This website contains two databases filled with the creators' favorite movies, along with the directors who filmed them. You'll find that the database is user-friendly, allowing people to add and edit movies and directors as they see fit. There is also a member's page with a list of the website's creators. If you want to search for movies quickly, we recommend browsing through the Movie List. It's a condensed page that only shows movie titles, and will also show the synopsis of whatever movie you click on. If you're looking for a more detailed view - or want to add something yourself - we recommend the Movie Directory.
